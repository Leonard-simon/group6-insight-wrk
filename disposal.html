NATIONAL ENVIRONMENT MANAGEMENT AUTHORITY (NEMA) COMPLIANCE:
• All pharmaceutical waste must be disposed of according to NEMA guidelines (Statutory Instrument No. 12 of 2023)
• Incineration temperature must exceed 800°C for complete destruction
• Disposal certificates must be kept for minimum 5 years for audit purposes
• For controlled substances, additional DEA/NDA reporting required
